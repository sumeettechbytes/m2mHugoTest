About Course

Through our hands-on-workshops, Tech Bytes team is committed to empowering youths with much needed digital literacy skills. Throughout this school year, we will conduct workshops on topics like Animation, Gaming, Graphics, Digital Marketing, Data Visualization, Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things, Web/App Development.

Our intermediate level series of Web Design is Web Design & Javascript.

Start Date: Oct 05 2021

End Date: Dec 16 2021

Time: Every Tuesday & Wednesday between 6 pm - 7 pm PDT/PST. Approximately 25 hours for this whole series.

In this workshop, we will go through the basics of Javascript and few hands on projects on creating stunning websites for business.

Cost of this workshop series - 60 CAD/month - 180 CAD/3 months.

For questions regarding registration please drop us an email at contact@m2mtechconnect.com .

Requirements

  • No prior knowledge on this topic is required for this workshop.
  • Students need a laptop (tablet/phones are not recommended) with internet connection for attending this online workshop.
  • Targeted age group for this workshop is students of age group 12-18.
